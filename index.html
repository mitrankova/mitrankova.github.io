ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Iurii Mitrankov, Research Scholar in the area of elementary particle and high energy physics.

I have studied physics of atomic nuclei and elementary particles physics in the St. Petersburg Polytechnic univercity (SPbPU) in bachelor, master, and PhD programs.

During the study, I've entered the PHENIX collaboration at RHIC collider. I did an analysis devoted to phi meson production in Cu+Au and U+U collisions.

Besides my own scientific research, I work as a research assistant in SPbPU and give a course of lections to students from 5 to 8 sem on QFT and also practices devoted to high energy physics packages. Under my guideness 5 students of SPbPU are proceeding whith thier analysis for the PHENIX collaboration.
